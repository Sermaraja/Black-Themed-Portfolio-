# Collaboration Champion Award (2025 – 26)

## Overview
The **Collaboration Champion Award** is the highest internal recognition presented by **Devopstrio Ltd.** to acknowledge an individual who demonstrates exceptional teamwork, proactive peer support, technical guidance, and knowledge sharing.

## Reason for Award
Sermaraja V was honored with this award for:
- **Knowledge Sharing & Training**: Actively organizing and conducting internal technical workshops on cloud migration tools, PowerShell scripting, and system monitoring.
- **Cross-Functional Support**: Assisting junior engineers and cross-departmental teams in debugging complex infrastructure issues and deployment bottlenecks.
- **Team Culture**: Cultivating an encouraging, collaborative work environment where innovation and continuous learning thrive.
